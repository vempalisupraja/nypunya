NYPUNYA-2K19

A National Level Students Inter Engineering Collegiate Sports Meet

29 th & 30 th Jan 2019

General Rules:

  • All the competitions will be conducted only for women.
  • All the draws /fixtures and the events schedule will be given at the help desk.
  • Students pursuing B.Tech, M.Tech, M.B.A only are eligible to participate in the competitions.
  • No common players for the three events (Volley Ball, Throw Ball, Kho – Kho and Kabaddi) as these matches are organized simultaneously.
  • Teams and players must report 30 minutes in advance to the scheduled match time.
  • All the participants must carry their college ID cards without fail.
  • Mandatory for all participating teams to bring their own sports kit / uniform for all the events.
  • The Volley Ball,Throw ball and kabaddi teams can have 10 players each and Kho-Kho can have 12 players.
  • All the chess participants must carry their own chess boards.
  • The decisions of the referees and the sports committee are final and binding. No request will be entertained.
  • No match will be re-scheduled.
  • Announced Prize Money will be given only if minimum of 6 teams or 8 participants turn up. If not mementoes and certificates will be given.
  • We also request you to carry an Institution Flag for march-past.
  • The Inauguration of the meet will be at 9.30 AM on 29th January 2019.
  • Our college is a Women’s college, we request the participating colleges not to allow their boys to accompany them to avoid unwanted situations.
  • Please visit our college website www.bvrithyderabad.edu.in
